About Us

Regular Folks

We’re Midwesterners born and bred. We love to travel and explore but we also work full time, have families, pay rent, and have student loans. We travel on a budget—sometimes saving and sometimes splurging. Time and money are limited so we try to make the most of our travels. We’ve learned a lot along the way and are still learning. We want to share what we’ve learned to help you make the most of your travels too!

Quite a Pair

We each have skills and weaknesses that, thankfully, seem to compliment each other when we’re planning and on the road. Read on to learn a little more.

Our Travel Philosophy

Walk, live as local as we can, respect local customs, be sustainable. The best place to observe and meet locals is at the pub.
